Baking recipe

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Mix ingredients together
  3. Place ingredients in a baking dish

Before you place the ingredients in the baking dish, preheat the oven to 180 degrees centigrade/350 degrees fahrenheit in readiness for the next step.

  1. Bake in oven for an hour
  2. Remove from oven
  3. Allow to stand for ten minutes
    1. Make sure the cake is well done
    2. Check with a tooth pick or paring knife in the center to see if crumb is set
    3. If not, add some more time in oven
    4. Layer with desired frosting
      5.
  4. Serve

Ingredients

  • 3 Bananas
  • 3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs
